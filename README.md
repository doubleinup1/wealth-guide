# Building Wealth in a Shrinking-Dollar World — A Field Guide

A self-contained HTML field guide covering the principles, assets, and account strategies
for building durable wealth in a fiat-currency era.

**Read the guide:** open `index.html` in any browser, or visit the published GitHub Pages link.

## Contents
- Forces that erode wealth (inflation, taxation, fiat currency)
- The cash foundation: buckets and emergency funds
- Assets vs liabilities
- The five wealth-building assets (stocks, real estate, gold & silver, Bitcoin, cash)
- Account architecture (Roth IRA, 401(k), HSA, brokerage) and what to hold inside each
- Compound interest and dollar-cost averaging
- The FIRE math
- Surviving drawdowns
- Trusts and estate planning

Educational overview. Not personalized financial advice.
